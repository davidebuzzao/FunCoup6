The full format is a tab-separated value (TSV) format with the following columns:

ColumnLabelDescription
0-1Protein*UniProt identifiers of the proteins in the pair
2-3FunCoup*idThe internal FunCoup identifiers for the proteins
4GoldStandardThe gold standard network with the maximum confidence for the link
5PPVThe PPV confidence score of the link
6FBS_maxThe maximum Final Bayesian Score
7isGoldStandardDenotes links that are in the gold standards with 1 and links that do not appear in the gold standards with 0
8isPPVgoldstandardDenotes links that have a gold standard-based PPV with 1 and links that have an evidence-based PPV with 0
9GRG_directionThe direction of the link from the GRG evidence
10-15FBS_*The FBS scores of the different coupling classes (Some species don't have all classes)
16-24LLR_*The log-likelihood ratios (LLRs) of the different evidence types
25-46LLR_*The LLRs of the evidence from the different species
